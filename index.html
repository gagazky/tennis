Among the hundreds we meet, we select a group of Indonesia's most promising young players — our Top Seeds.

For these exceptional few, Top Seeds serves as a stepping stone: a place to train, be mentored, and prepare for the next level.

OUTREACH PROGRAM

Our team at Top Seeds Initiative partners with local coaches to
share training methods, creating a ripple effect of better instruction

OUR PHILOSOPHY

WE ARE THE BIRTHPLACE OF MINDFUL TENNIS.

We’re not just training athletes — we’re shaping humans.
Through mindfulness, discipline, and heart, our players
learn to show up on the court and in life with clarity,
confidence, and compassion.

OUR GOALS

Our goal is to guide each of them into the Mouratoglou Academy — whether in France, Malaysia, or any of their other global academies. From there, they’ll be positioned to pursue a college tennis scholarship or launch onto the professional tour. Top Seeds provides the foundation — Mouratoglou builds the future.

SUPPORTED BY
PRANA SHALA BALI

The Top Seeds Initiative, fully supported by Prana Shala Bali, is founded on the belief that tennis is more than a sport — it’s a pathway to growth, discipline, and opportunity. As a foundation and tennis & wellness retreat in the heart of Ubud, we are a sanctuary for sport, community, and holistic wellbeing. Through this program, we give back by honoring the spirit of tennis and investing in the next generation of Indonesian talent, both on and off the court.
